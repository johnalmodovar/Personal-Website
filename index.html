About Me

Based in Los Angeles, California — I pursued a marketing degree in Mt. San Antonio College while working in the food industry with 10 years of experience in multiple managerial positions before transitioning into software engineering.

A close friend encouraged me to make the switch into software engineering because he believed I had the talent and determination to succeed in this field and have an inherent passion for computers. I decided to give his idea a try by studying JavaScript and the basics of coding — the more I immersed myself into the fundamentals of coding, the more I realized this is the career I truly wanted to pursue.

Throughout the years of my tenure in the food industry, I have held a managerial position throughout my jobs, where I refined and developed my skillets to successfully lead my teams to complete their tasks with efficiency and poise. These skillsets included:

  • Team Leadership: guiding and motivating team members to achieve efficiency and a positive work environment.
  • Operational Management: overseeing daily operations to ensure efficiency and addressing issues that occur.
  • Communication: transparancy, actively listening to team members and providing feedback to others while being open to receiving feedbacks.

My goals for the future are to become a full stack engineer and work for companies to create innovate apps, websites, and other cutting-edge projects.

In my spare time, I love to watch anime, play video games with friends, and hang out with my cat.
